Who is redize??

redize is an elusive, internet-born music project that exists almost entirely through streaming platforms,
with little to no confirmed information about the person or people behind it.
Operating in the space between underground rap, alternative pop, and hyperpop-adjacent sounds, the project releases short,
fragmented tracks that feel like transmissions rather than traditional songs.
There is no public identity, no documented origin story, and no clear
narrative—only a growing catalog of music that appears, circulates, and disappears into algorithmic playlists.
This lack of presence outside of the music itself has turned redize into something intentionally hard to define: not quite an artist in the
conventional sense, but a shifting digital signature that leaves more questions than answers.
